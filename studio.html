Muscle Memory Musik Studio

Founded in 2005, Muscle Memory Musik has been a hub for artists, producers, and collectives — supporting growth, development, and collaborative work within the community.

Studio History

2005

Studio founded and built from the ground up.

Early Foundations

Producer Young Max becomes a pivotal collaborator.

2012

Dishy Tangent formed by Reason and Matt Shilk.

2014

Sing Out Loud Sundays founded by RM Moses; studio serves as HQ.

2019

New collaborations with Swayla and others; contemporary sounds evolve.

Present

Ladder Records emerges as a creative collective.

Dishy Tangent

Founded in 2012 by Reason and Matt Shilk, Dishy Tangent explored genre-fluid songwriting and live-led production.

After Matt left, Reason collaborated with Sustainard (2014), Sonic & Dr Dish (2015), and Yung Blud (2018).

Recent releases include Clear (2021) and W.I.L.D. (2022).
