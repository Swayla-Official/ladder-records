Muscle Memory Musik Studio

Muscle Memory Musik is an independent recording studio founded in 2005. Built and rebuilt over time, the studio has evolved through movement, collaboration, and hands-on creative work.

More than a recording space, the studio functions as a development hub — supporting artists, producers, and collectives at different stages of their journey.

Studio History

2005

Muscle Memory Musik Studio is founded. Built from the ground up with creative freedom at the core.

Early Foundations

Producers & Engineers Young Max, Del aka Sonic were pivotal in early collaborations and formed the initial trio of producers and engineers of Muscle Memory Studio alongside Louis aka Reason..

2012

Dishy Tangent is formed by Reason and Matt Shilk.

2014

Sing Out Loud Sundays is founded by RM Moses. The studio becomes HQ for rehearsals and filmed sessions.

2019

Collaboration with Swayla begins, marking a new wave of UKG and electronic productions.

Present

Ladder Records emerges as a collective from this ecosystem of artists and studio projects.
