Muscle Memory Musik Studio

Founded in 2005, Muscle Memory Musik Studio has grown from a hands-on creative space to a thriving artist development hub. From live recording to producing full albums, we support artists at all stages of their journey.

Studio History

2005

Muscle Memory Musik Studio is founded and built from the ground up.

Early Foundations

Producer Young Max becomes a pivotal early collaborator.

2012

Dishy Tangent is formed by Reason and Matt Shilk.

2014

Sing Out Loud Sundays founded by RM Moses. Studio becomes HQ.

2019

Collaborations with Swayla and others begin.

Present

Ladder Records collective emerges.

Dishy Tangent

Dishy Tangent blends live instrumentation with modern production. Recent releases include Clear (2021) and W.I.L.D. (2022).
